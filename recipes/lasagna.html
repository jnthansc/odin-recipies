Ingredients

  • Lasagna pasta
  • Tomatoes
  • Vegan cheese
  • Vegan ground beef

Steps

  1. Dice the tomatoes
  2. Fry the ground beef in a pan to get some crust with extra flavor
  3. Add the tomatoes to loosen the roast flavor from the bottom of the pan
  4. Put everything together in a form and place it in the oven for 30 minutes
