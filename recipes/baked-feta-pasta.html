Ingredients

  • Feta
  • Cherry-tomatoes
  • Pasta
  • Garlic
  • Thyme
  • Honey
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Preaheat the oven
  2. Put the tomatoes and thyme into a form and mix it with the oil
  3. Place the feta in the middle of the form
  4. Add the honey on top of the feta
  5. Put everything in the oven for half an hour
  6. In the meanwhile cook the pasta
  7. After the feta is ready, add it to the pasta and mix well
